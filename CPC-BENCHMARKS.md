## Bidding Strategy Decision Matrix

| Condition | Strategy | Why |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| No conversions tracked | DON'T LAUNCH | Set up tracking first |
| < 15 conversions/month | Manual CPC + ECPC | Smart Bidding needs data |
| 15-50 conversions/month | Maximize Conversions → Target CPA | Enough data for automation |
| 50+ conversions/month + ecommerce | Target ROAS | Revenue optimization |
| Broad match testing | Only with Smart Bidding + 30+ conv/month | Needs conversion signals |
| New account, first 30 days | Manual CPC + phrase match | Learn your search terms first |

## Quality Score Impact

QS improvement from 5 → 7 on a $5.00 CPC keyword saves approximately **$0.75-1.00 per click**. Across 1,000 clicks/month = $750-1,000/month saved.

QS components:
- Expected CTR (most important)
- Ad relevance
- Landing page experience
